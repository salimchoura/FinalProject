How to Foody

Welcome to Foody, the social media site for foodies!

At Foody, we want to make sure you have the best experience possible on our website, and we don't want our website's structure to get in the way of your cooking enjoyment. If you need help with any of our features, you've come to the right place. Below you can find a simple how-to guide for each and every one of our website's features. Happy cooking!


Navigation

At the top of every page, you can find a handy navigation bar with direct access to every page of the website. To visit the Home page, click on "Home." To log in or sign up for an account, click on "Sign Up / Login." To access this page, click "Help." To access our forum, click "Explore forum." Finally, to find your next favorite recipe, click "Explore recipes." Each one of these pages may connect to other pages that you can only find there; these pages will be described in their respective sections.


Accounts

To sign up for an account, simply enter a username and password in the "Create Account" section of our login page. This also appears when you first navigate to our website, although you can click on any other page in the navigation bar if you would prefer to continue as a guest. However, if you are not logged in with your account, you cannot review recipes, add forum posts, or share recipes. To log in, simply type your registered username and password into the respective fields on the login page (these will be in the "Login" section). That's it! Now you have full access to all of Foody.


Home Page

The home page displays a slideshow of foods. You can swipe to the left or right to see other delicious foods. Maybe they can be inspiration for your next yummy creation!


Exploring Recipes

On the "Explore recipes" page, you will find a search bar, which you can use to search for recipes by their title. There is also a button for you to add your own recipe, but we will get to that later. Just use the search bar to type in a keyword (or many keywords). Your search results will appear as images, which you can click on to see the recipe used to create the food in the image. Once you click on the image, you will be taken to the recipe's page, where you can see how to make it, the ingredients you'll need, and some nutrition facts. You can also see reviews. A bar chart is shown displaying the overall distribution of the reviews, and users' comments are displayed below. You can even provide your own review, or update a review you left previously (provided that you are logged in, of course). Just click on the star representing your review (for example, click on the fourth star from the left to give a four-star review) and write a comment, and then click "post!"


Adding a Recipe

If you're logged in, you can add your own recipes. But be careful! If you are not logged in, then you will be redirected back to the login page so you can log in and post your delicious treats. When you add a recipe, you will be asked for your recipe's title, the grams of protein, carbs, and fat present, the recipe instructions, and an image to go along with your recipe. In addition, you can specify the ingredients in your recipe. You can add as many ingredients as you'd like, but make sure to click on "Add ingredient" as many times as there are ingredients in your recipe before you add all of your ingredients. You cannot add on new ones as you go along. Finally, once you're done creating your recipe, click "Create Post" to add your recipe to our website. You can go back up to the navigation bar and click "Explore recipes" and then search for your recipe to admire the awesome work you just did!
